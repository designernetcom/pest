Our Services Assessments

We follow the ERD principle (Exclusion-Restriction-Destruction), The philosophy being that prenevtion is always better than cure.


We work in a partnership with you to help maintain a pest free environment through effective implementation of the ERD process.

Exclusion

Prevent a pest from entering in premises.

Restriction

Preventing a site becoming hospitable tp pests.

Destruction

Eradication or removal of the infestation.
